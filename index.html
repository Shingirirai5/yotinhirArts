Yotinhira Arts Private Limited. Est 2008.

Proffesional Marimba and Mbira makers located at 8053, Cold comfort, Harare. We supply traditional music instruments including but not limited to:

  • Mbira of all kinds and sizes such as Nyunganyunga, Nhare and Njari.
  • Mbira of all tunnings and sizes including standard, extended and chromatic.
  • Other percussion instruments and dance props such as nheme, magavhu, ngoma & hosho.

Our Mission

To promote Africanicity through the provision of traditional and unique instruments, related supplies, music education, and music promotion or advocacy with particular emphasis on Zimbabwean traditional instrument fusions.

Our Vision

To become a go to hub for traditional music enthusiasts and inspire greater participation in musical arts at all levels.
Our vision is to provide opportunity and cultivate cultural experiences through the manufacture of traditional instruments, teaching or learning to play the respective instruments, and developing appreciation for traditional art at local, regional, and global levels.

Our Values

To promote a culture of respect that is nurturing for african music enthusiasts of all levels.
We believe inclusiveness and diversity enrich learning
We value community and seek to support and collaborate with all positive efforts towards cultural enhancement in conjunction with businesses, schools, organizations, trusts and individuals.

Marimba Manufacturing

Yotinhira 579 standard ensamble Marimba set with half dozen (Pair Soprano, Pair Tenor, 1 baritone and 1 bass) pieces.
Each instrument covers 2 Octaves, Making a cummulative 6 octaves (C to C6).
Only the finest Mukwa (Pterocarpus angolensis) is used in the construction which guarantees full and carrying ground tones even in the low range.
The Yotinhira 579 Marimba set allows very dynamic play with exceptional balance in every position.
It features a reinforced frame and a unique adjustable and foldable height system, perfect for the younger percussionist.
The frames have been exquisitely crafted of natural-finish wood and strong, but lightweight, metal.
They can be dismantled, folded and reassembled easily and quickly, making transport from place to place very easy.
Perfect, consistent Intonation.
Buzzer system for resonance and amplified, authentic sound quality.
High-quality thermoplastic Polyvinyl Chloride and fibreglass resonators.
Perfect resonator synchronization without adjustment.
Pluggable clips to stabilize resonator beam rows and for easy dismantling and transportation.

Band Coaching

We offer engaging private and group lessons for marimba, mbira, and percussion for all age-groups.
